About Me

This is what I do.

I am a Legacy Application Developer with more than 11 years of professional experience building and enhancing enterprise systems. I enjoy solving complex problems through clean code and thoughtful design.

My curiosity keeps me learning, which recently inspired me to dive into Python. I use it to experiment with automation ideas, personal tools and projects that push me beyond my Mainframe background. BreakTime Stories is one of those projects. I designed and built this blog myself using Python, Flask and Jinja, turning our break-time conversations about current affairs, cinema, sports and everything in between into a space where those stories can live and grow.

Outside of work, I like to keep life active and interesting. You will often find me playing cricket, badminton, volleyball or heading out for a cycling session on weekends. I am a firm believer in balancing tech with time outdoors.

I enjoy reading nonfiction, especially biographies and books that teach me something new about the real world. I also love watching documentaries, feel good movies, action films and sci-fi stories that spark imagination and inspiration.

I approach everything with a mindset of continuous improvement. Learning new skills, staying physically active and exploring creative hobbies all help me become a better version of myself, one step at a time!
